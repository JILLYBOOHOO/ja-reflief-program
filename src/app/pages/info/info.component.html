🛡️ What To Do In An Emergency

🔥

If There Is a Fire

  • Stay calm and leave the building immediately.
  • Use stairs instead of elevators.
  • Stay low if there is smoke.
  • Call emergency services (110).
  • Use a fire extinguisher only if the fire is small and safe to control.
🌍

If There Is an Earthquake

  • Drop to the ground.
  • Cover your head and neck.
  • Hold on until the shaking stops.
  • Stay away from windows and heavy objects.
  • Move to an open area after the shaking stops.
🌧️

During Flooding

  • Move to higher ground immediately.
  • Do not walk or drive through flood water.
  • Follow evacuation instructions from authorities.
  • Stay informed through emergency alerts.
