⚠️ Safety Tips During Emergency

🌧️ During Flooding

  • Move to higher ground immediately
  • Avoid walking/driving through flood waters
  • Turn off electricity at main switch

🌀 During Hurricane

  • Stay indoors away from windows
  • Keep emergency kit accessible
  • Listen to battery-powered radio for updates

🏚️ If Trapped

  • Text 119 if you can't call
  • Bang on pipes or walls
  • Use whistle if available
