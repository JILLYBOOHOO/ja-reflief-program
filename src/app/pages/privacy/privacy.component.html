Privacy Statement

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

At JA Relief, we collect your personal and contact information solely to facilitate disaster assistance. This includes your name, parish, physical address, and occasionally official ID documentation for provisional verification.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly to coordinate deliveries of food, water, and medical supplies to your specified address. We do not sell or distribute your data to third-party marketers.

3. Cookie Policy

We use local browser storage (Cookies and LocalStorage) to save your session data, keep you logged into the dashboard, and record your consent preferences. Disabling cookies will break the login functionality of the app.

4. Essential Security Data

Stripe and PayPal process all monetary donations. We do not store your full credit card information on our servers. All transactions are securely handled by the payment processors.
